## Call for Participation

The gem5 Workshop at [ISCA 2025](https://iscaconf.org/isca2025/) brought together the community for a full day of discussions, presentations, and collaboration centered around the gem5 computer architecture simulator. The event featured presentations on research, new features, and community developments.
